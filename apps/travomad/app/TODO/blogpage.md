## 📋 CATEGORY ORGANIZATION STRATEGY

### **How to Assign Categories:**

**Primary Category** (one per post):
- Main topic/content type

**Secondary Tags** (multiple allowed):
- Destinations
- Subtopics
- Travel style

**Example:**
```
Article: "Best All-Inclusive Resorts in Mexico"

Primary Category: Hotels & Resorts
Tags: Mexico, Luxury Travel, Beach Vacations, All-Inclusive
```
